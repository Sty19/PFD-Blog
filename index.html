Week Two, Oct 23 2023

Posted on October 31, 2023 by Shawn Tey

On the second week of our PFD class, my team and I ideated around, trying to decide on the type of technology we would be using in order to solve the problem statement that had been presented to us. We had settled on the idea of using RFID tags, along with bluetooth technology, to track employees attendances automatically without much intraction having to be done by the user. Although the idea is still raw, we had been on it together through the coming days to iron out any flaws that may have been present with the idea.

Week Three, Oct 30 2023

Posted on November 8, 2023 by Shawn Tey

For the third week of PFD class, we continued to make progress on each team members roles and responsibilities for the project, allowing us to carry out the work we needed to do more effectively. We had also spent time trying and testing out our Arduino board, still grasping how we could utilise it for our project. Overall there was good progress made during this week and the outlook for our work is looking good.

Week Four, Nov 6 2023

Posted on November 8, 2023 by Shawn Tey

For the fourth week of PFD, we dabbled heavily in the engineering side of our project, even with the help of Mr Ben, we struggled to undersatnd the requirments neccessary for our little Arduino board to function as intended, leading to multiple failed attempts to fix all the issues that appeared. Luckily for us, our persistence did lead to a solution by the end of the lesson time, giving us hope to build on it for the hackathon next week. Personally, I did my research on the software required to 3D model a gantry, one of the things we are hoping to showcase next week along with our little gadget. After much frustration trying to use AutoCAD, we compomised and will betrying to build a successful example of what the gantry may look like using cardboard.
